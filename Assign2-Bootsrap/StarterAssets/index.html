About Us

Pan Pacific Hotels and Resorts can be found along the west coast of North America, in Australia and across Asia, from as far south as Bali to as north as China.Each property provides an invigorating blend of the best that the Pacific has to offer. The service and traditions of the east combine with the creativity and personality of the west to provide refreshing experiences that leave you feeling restored and rejuvenated.

Our Location

Pacific Hotel,317 Marion St, Seattle, WA 98104, USA

Pacific Hotel Group offers spacious guestrooms, value added amenities, and friendly service at any of our locations in San Jose, Redwood City, and Santa Maria.

Whether you are on business or visiting Silicon Valley, come experience our complimentary breakfast and dinner, relax in our 4 foot Jacuzzi tubs, and meet our exceptional staff ready to make your next stay a memorable one. In line with the brand's plans to expand the Pacific Hotels and Resorts portfolio in Asia, Greater China and Oceania, Pan Pacific debuted in China with Pan Pacific Xiamen in 2009 and Pan Pacific Suzhou in the following year. The brand also extended its footprint into Australia with the launch of Pan Pacific Perth in 2011.

From the mountain ranges and majestic forests of the Pacific Northwest to the tropical archipelagos of the South Pacific and beaches of Australia, the richness of the Pacific Rim is brought to life in sensorial experiences that are both exquisite and enriching.

It established the Emerald Management Company (EMC) to manage its hotels in Hawaii and California in 1983, and in the same year, the Traveller's Palm logo was born. In 1986, Pan Pacific Vancouver and Pan Pacific Singapore were opened. Tokyu Hotels International and EMC merged to become Pan Pacific Hotels and Resorts, and in 1989, the Traveller's Palm logo became the company-wide mark for the hotel group.
